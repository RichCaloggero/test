Hooray for Hyphen­ation

This text contains some Hyphen­ation. Hope­fully it is not in­com­pre­hen­sible. Usually NVDA can process soft hyphens in documents pretty well, but did you notice the previous word "incomprehensible"?

Pay attention to the word "pronunciation", which is pronunced differently with soft hyphens in it:

With: "pro­nun­cia­tion"

Without: "pronunciation"

Pro­nun­cia­tion is the key to under­standing the spoken word

Did you know? Soft hyphens have been around since the 80s!
